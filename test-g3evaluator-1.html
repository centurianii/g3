/*
 * 1) Open drop down panel "Add/Remove .css/.js library" and 
 *    add resources
 *
 * 2) Open drop down panel "Load .css/.js library" and click 
 *    to load resources or,uncheck to remove resources.
 *    If you uncheck "Keep list frozen" then your visual 
 *    list order is re-arranged to the library source order 
 *    as you check or uncheck libraries from the list,
 *    i.e. the 3rd check from the 6th library is moved 3rd 
 *    on the list, the 4th becomes 4th on the list etc.
 *    Our goal is to have an exact view of the actual source
 *    order.
 *
 * 3) Open drop down panel "Add/Remove panel" and type the 
 *    title of the panel you want to add
 *
 * 4) Go to "Blackboard" write a title and some code to
 *    execute; when you are satisfied click on the panel
 *    you want this code to be saved and return at 
 *    "Blackboard" and pres save with button
 *    "Save to a new tab". Repeat as many times you want
 *
 * 5) To load a tab from your panels click the tab title
 *    and press button "Load tab" at the right side of
 *    the panel's title; go to "Blackboard" to see your
 *    code loaded and ready to be executed
 *
 * 6) To modify an existed tab of a panel, load it as
 *    explained in step (5), modify it and press button
 *    "Save" of the "Blackboard" to save the modified 
 *    version; if you highlight another tab while 
 *    you have still on "Blackboard" the previous one
 *    you'll see a clickable error message; the system
 *    tries to find a unique title among the existed
 *    ones at the panel; just click on the message,
 *    correct the title to be the same as the tab 
 *    you are interested, highlight that tab and then
 *    press "Save" at the "Blackboard"; always the 
 *    title of the "Blackboard" should be the same as
 *    that of the highlighted tab to activate "Save"
 *
 * 7) Panels form 2 columns but if you check the label
 *    "Single column panels" just below "Add/Remove
 *    panel" you'll have single column panels that
 *    stretch the entire screen
 *
 * 8) When you want to save the entire page first
 *    - check the tabs you want to be activated on 
 *      page reload, 
 *    - then close other opened drop down panels 
 *      like "Add/Remove .js library" etc. and
 *    - finally, open drop down "Load html", press
 *      button "Load" and you can copy the actual
 *      page contents from the textarea above
 */
